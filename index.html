Data Analyzed: World Happiness Report 2016 and Internet Users By Country 2016

QUESTION POSED

The Happiness score and rankings (by countries) use data from the Gallup World Poll. The scores are based on asnwers to the main life evaluation question asked in the poll in each country. This question, known as the Cantril ladder, asks respondents to think of a ladder with worst to best possibly life being from 0 to 10, and to rate their own current lives on that scale.

Key points discussed/analyzed: Economic Production(GDP), Social Support, Life Expectancy, Freedom, Absence of Corruption, and Generosity.

Conclusion




The data of the 152 countries across the globe showed very strong correlations for the following factors:
Health Life expectancy Vs. % internet usage
Happiness Score Vs. % internet usage
GDP per capita Vs. % internet penetration

There was no correlation between Generosity Vs. internet usage

Though correlation isn't causation, the internet usage has played a role in improving the overall quality of life. The internet helps in enriching one's social life, as he/she can communicate with people easily and share pictures, thoughts, etc.
